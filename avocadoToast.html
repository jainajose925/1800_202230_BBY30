How to make Avocado Toast

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole milk ricotta cheese or Whipped Ricotta
  • Kosher salt
  • Fresh ground pepper
  • Slices of bread
  • Ripe avocado
  • Radishes
  • Garlic salt

Instructions

  1. Stir together the ricotta with Â¼ teaspoon kosher salt and several grinds of fresh ground pepper; taste and adjust seasoning as desired. Or, make the Whipped Ricotta: place 16 ounces whole milk ricotta, 3 tablespoons milk and 1 tablespoon olive oil in a bowl and whisk with a whisk for about 2 minutes until light and fluffy.
  2. Toast the bread.
  3. Spread ricotta on the toast.
  4. Top the toast with sliced radishes, drizzle of olive oil, and a small pinch salt and fresh ground pepper.
